Send, Receive, and Exchange Coins With Zelcore Wallet

Send and Receive Any Listed Coin

Send and receive any listed coin or asset easily with an address or scannable QR code. Each Zelcore account comes standard with 3 addresses per asset, of which you and only you have the private keys. The Zelcore wallet supports 100+ other coins and ERC-20 tokens, and is available for desktop and mobile devices.

Buy and sell assets via Built-in Exchanges*

Zelcore integrates built-in quickswap exchanges directly inside of Zelcore Desktop & Mobile. For more advanced traders, checkout Zelcore+ which offers a full API integration of some of your favourite exchanges such as Binance, Bittrex and Kraken.
(*) if coin is available on exchange

Store your coins 100% Secure with ZelID, a decentralized authentication protocol that is truly seamless, secure, and private. A digital identity you control for cross-platform authentication.
